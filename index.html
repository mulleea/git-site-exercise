Covid Symptoms and Prevention

Symptoms

  • Fever or Chills
  • Cough
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Headache
  • Muscle or Body Aches
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion
  • Nausea

Seek Medical Care for:

  • Trouble Breathing
  • Persisten pain in chest
  • Confusion
  • Inability to stay awake
  • Blue lips or face

Prevention

  • Wash your hands
  • Wear a mask when in public
  • Monitor temperature and health
  • Do not touch your face
  • Limmit close-contact with others
  • Socially distance
